Madison County Website Collaboration

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: Madison County
  • Project date: May 1, 2024

With a team of 11 students, from two majors, Interactive Design as well as Computer Information Systems, we competed against our classmates to redesign the Madison County administrative website. We created a mockup system to organize their administrative documents and information, as well as ensure optimal usability for users aged 40-70.
