  • Category: Munch
  • Professor: Chen Guo
  • Project date: October, 2022

To develop user research skills, we were tasked with creating an app to solve a problem within the Harrisonburg, VA community, whether that be on campus or in town. In Harrisonburg, it can be quite difficult to discover restaurants off campus for first year students, which was why I decided to create an app, with competitors such as yelp, to help students connect with each other and discover hot new dining options. The app covers the bases on how long it takes to get to the restaurant, the menu options, reviews and questions for other students, potential student discounts, and much more.
